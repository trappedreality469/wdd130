White Water Rafting Company

We provide thrilling and safe white water rafting adventures for all skill levels. Our experienced guides lead unforgettable trips on scenic rivers, with all gear provided. Only the best locations, the most fun waters, and only smiling faces year-round.

History

Founded to share the love of rivers and excitement of rafting, our company has guided thousands of guests through rapids and calm stretches alike. We focus on safety, stewardship of waterways, and creating memories.

We’ve been committed, since 1997, to delivering unforgettable whitewater adventures. Our American adventures are truly the heat of the moment!
